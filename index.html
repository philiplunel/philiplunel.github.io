Philip Verduyn Lunel

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Sorbonne Université in the group of Alex Grilo. I work on quantum information theory, quantum cryptography, and non-local quantum computation, with a particular focus on quantum position verification. Previously I was a PhD student at CWI & Qusoft in Amsterdam, supervised by Harry Buhrman and Florian Speelman.
